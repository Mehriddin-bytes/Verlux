# Verlux Website Plan

## Navigation Structure
**Navbar:** Home | Services | Projects | About Us | Pricing | Contact Us

---

## Page Sections & Features

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero Section**
  - Large hero image background (construction site)
  - Compelling headline (e.g., "Building Your Dreams, One Brick at a Time")
  - Call-to-action buttons (Get Free Quote, View Projects)
  - Trust indicators (Years of Experience, Projects Completed)

- **Services Overview**
  - Grid of main services with icons
  - Quick preview cards linking to Services page

- **Featured Projects**
  - Showcase 3-4 recent/completed projects
  - Image gallery with project details
  - "View All Projects" button

- **Why Choose Us**
  - Key differentiators (Licensed, Insured, Quality Materials, etc.)
  - Icons with brief descriptions

- **Testimonials**
  - Customer reviews with photos
  - Star ratings
  - Client names and project types

- **CTA Section**
  - "Ready to Start Your Project?" with contact form or button

## Technical Requirements
- **Responsive Design**
  - Mobile-first approach
  - Tablet and desktop optimization

- **Performance**
  - Fast loading times
  - Optimized images
  - Lazy loading for galleries

- **SEO**
  - Meta tags
  - Structured data
  - Local SEO optimization

- **Accessibility**
  - WCAG compliance
  - Keyboard navigation
  - Screen reader support

## Design Considerations
- **Color Scheme**
  - Professional (blues, grays, whites)

- **Imagery**
  - High-quality construction photos
  - Professional team photos
  - Before/after project images
